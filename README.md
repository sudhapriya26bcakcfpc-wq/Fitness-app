# 📱 Pulse – Fitness & Wellness App

## Overview

**Pulse** is a modern fitness and wellness mobile application designed to help users build healthier habits every day. The app brings together fitness tracking, nutrition, heart-rate monitoring, sleep tracking, and wellness insights in one easy-to-use platform.

**Tagline:**
**"Build healthier habits, one day at a time."**

---

# 🎯 Project Goal

The goal of Pulse is to motivate users to live healthier by providing personalized fitness tracking, workout guidance, nutrition monitoring, and wellness management in a simple and engaging interface.

---

# 👥 Target Users

* Fitness beginners
* Active gym members
* People tracking their health
* Users improving nutrition
* Individuals building daily healthy habits
